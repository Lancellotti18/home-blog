Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 12, 2026

The short version: We don't sell your data. We collect basic info (like your email if you subscribe) to run this site and send you guides you asked for. We use Amazon affiliate links — clicking them doesn't cost you anything extra.

1. Who We Are

The House Hunter ("we," "us," or "our") is a family-focused affiliate blog at home-blog.vercel.app. We publish product guides and home improvement content for families. You can reach us at hello@thehousehunter.com.

2. Information We Collect

Information you give us

  • Email address — only if you sign up for our newsletter. We use this to send you weekly home finds and guides.
  • Contact messages — if you reach out to us, we keep your message and email to reply.

Information collected automatically

  • Analytics data — we may use Google Analytics or similar tools to see which articles are popular. This data is anonymous and aggregated.
  • Cookies — basic cookies may be set by our hosting platform or analytics tools. You can disable cookies in your browser settings.

3. Amazon Affiliate Disclosure

The House Hunter is a participant in the Amazon Services LLC Associates Program, an affiliate advertising program designed to provide a means for sites to earn advertising fees by advertising and linking to Amazon.com.

This means that when you click a product link on our site and make a purchase on Amazon, we may earn a small commission — at no extra cost to you whatsoever. This is how we keep the site free and the guides coming.

We only recommend products we genuinely believe in. Our editorial opinions are never influenced by affiliate relationships.

4. How We Use Your Information

  • To send you the newsletter you signed up for
  • To reply to your messages
  • To understand which content is most helpful and improve our guides
  • We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes

5. Pinterest

We share our content on Pinterest. When you interact with our Pinterest pins, Pinterest's own privacy policy applies. We use Pinterest's API to automate pin publishing. No personal data from this website is shared with Pinterest beyond standard web traffic.

6. Third-Party Links

Our site contains links to Amazon and other third-party websites. Once you leave our site, their privacy policies apply. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of other sites.

7. Your Rights

You can:

  • Unsubscribe from our newsletter at any time using the unsubscribe link in any email
  • Request deletion of your email and any data we hold about you by emailing hello@thehousehunter.com
  • Opt out of analytics by using a browser extension like uBlock Origin

8. Children's Privacy

Our site is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. The "last updated" date at the top will always reflect the most recent version. Continued use of the site after changes means you accept the updated policy.

10. Contact Us

Questions about this policy? Email us at hello@thehousehunter.com. We're real people and we actually read our emails.
