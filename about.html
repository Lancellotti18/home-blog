Our Story

A Blog Built by a Real Mom,
for Real Families

The House Hunter exists because I got tired of buying products that looked amazing online and did nothing in real life. So I started actually testing them.

How The House Hunter Started

A few years ago, I opened my kitchen cabinet and — I kid you not — three different "revolutionary" vegetable choppers fell out. None of them worked well. None of them were worth what I paid. All of them came with glowing reviews and great Instagram photos.

I'm Sarah. I'm a mom of three kids (ages 7, 11, and 14), a homeowner, and someone who genuinely loves making a home feel warm, functional, and beautiful — without losing my mind or emptying my bank account doing it.

I started The House Hunter because I couldn't find a reliable source that just told me what actually works. Most product blogs felt like they were either written by robots who had never touched a mop, or they were just dressed-up Amazon lists with no real experience behind them.

I wanted something different. So I built it.

Every guide on The House Hunter is written from real experience — either I've tested it myself, a member of our team has, or we've done deep research into the products that thousands of real buyers have used and loved. We're honest about what we don't like. We tell you when a cheaper option is just as good. And we always try to explain why something matters for real family life — not just what its specs are.

How We Choose What to Recommend

We don't just pick the highest-rated product on Amazon and call it a day. Here's our actual process:

  • We research extensively — we read hundreds of real buyer reviews, flag common complaints, and look for patterns in what people love and hate.
  • We test when we can — for high-priority picks, a team member buys the product and uses it in a real home for at least 2–4 weeks.
  • We consider real family life — how easy is it to clean? Is it safe around kids? Will it survive being dropped?
  • We look at long-term value — something cheap that breaks in three months isn't a deal. We look for durability and longevity.
  • We're transparent about sponsored content — we clearly label any sponsored posts. Our organic recommendations are never influenced by brand relationships.
